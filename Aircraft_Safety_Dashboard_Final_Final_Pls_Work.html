CS329E Project: Assessing The Impact of Engineering and Regulatory Advancements on Mechanical-Failure Aircraft Accident Rate.

This project evaluates how advancements in aviation engineering, manufacturing, and regulatory policies have impacted mechanical-failure-related aircraft accidents over time, using data from sources such as the NTSB and FAA. By analyzing accident trends, injury outcomes, and contributing factors, the results suggest that most safety improvements come from preventing accident occurrence through better system design and control, rather than significantly reducing the severity once an accident happens.

Team 31: Sara Ibrahim, Khoi Pham, Vivian Peng.
